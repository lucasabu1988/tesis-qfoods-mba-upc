# Referencias Bibliográficas Clave

## Marcos Teóricos Fundacionales

### Pecking Order Theory
- Myers, S. C. (1984). The Capital Structure Puzzle. *Journal of Finance*, 39(3), 575–592.
- Myers, S. C., & Majluf, N. S. (1984). Corporate financing and investment decisions when firms have information that investors do not have. *Journal of Financial Economics*, 13(2), 187–221.
- Kumar, S. et al. (2020). Bibliometric review of 262 studies on capital structure in SMEs. *Journal of Business Research*.

### Teoría de la Agencia
- Jensen, M. C., & Meckling, W. H. (1976). Theory of the Firm: Managerial Behavior, Agency Costs and Ownership Structure. *Journal of Financial Economics*, 3(4), 305–360.
- Jensen, M. C. (1986). Agency costs of free cash flow. *American Economic Review*, 76(2), 323–329.
- Botero, I. et al. (2021). 2,287 Latin American family SMEs, 24 countries. Governance and performance.

### Socioemotional Wealth (SEW) — *Marco incorporado v12*
- Gómez-Mejía, L. R. et al. (2007). Socioemotional wealth and business risks in family-controlled firms. *Administrative Science Quarterly*, 52(1), 106–137.
- Jansen, E., Michiels, A., Voordeckers, W., & Steijvers, T. (2022). Financing decisions in private family firms: a family firm pecking order. *PMC / Frontiers in Psychology*.
- Hernández-Linares et al. (2023). Family firms in Latin America and VUCA context. *Journal of Family Business Strategy*.

### Stewardship Theory — *Marco incorporado v12*
- Davis, J. H., Schoorman, F. D., & Donaldson, L. (1997). Toward a stewardship theory of management. *Academy of Management Review*, 22(1), 20–47.
- Alrezqat, B., & Mahmoud, M. (2022). Stewardship in family vs. non-family firms. *ScienceDirect*.
- Silveira, A. et al. (2020). Stewardship-oriented culture and family firm performance in emerging economy. *Brazilian Administration Review (SciELO)*.

### Teoría de la Contingencia
- Otley, D. T. (1980). The contingency theory of management accounting. *Accounting, Organizations and Society*, 5(4), 413–428.
- Otley, D. T. (2016). The contingency theory of management accounting and control. *Management Accounting Research*, 31, 45–62.

### VBM / EVA
- Sharma, A. K., & Kumar, S. (2010). Economic Value Added (EVA) — literature review with 461 citations. *Global Business Review*.
- Stern, J., & Willett, J. (2014). A look back at the beginnings of EVA and value-based management.
- Chambi-Condori et al. (2025). Trade-Off Theory in Peruvian SMEs. WACC promedio 8.49–8.67%, ROIC promedio 3.07%.

### Teoría de Señalización
- Spence, M. (1973). Job Market Signaling. *Quarterly Journal of Economics*, 87(3), 355–374. (Nobel 2001)

### Resource-Based View (RBV)
- Barney, J. B. (1991). Firm resources and sustained competitive advantage. *Journal of Management*, 17(1), 99–120.

### Altman Z"-Score
- Altman, E. I., Hartzell, J., & Peck, M. (1995). Emerging Markets Corporate Bonds: A Scoring System.
- Altman, E. I. (2018). A fifty-year journey: How the Z-Score model continues to evolve.
- Sánchez-Almeyda et al. (2025). Z-Score EMS en 147 empresas alimentos Colombia 2016–2023.

### Crítica al CAPM
- Damodaran, A. (2005). Valuing private companies. NYU Stern.
- Damodaran, A. (2024). Equity Risk Premiums — annual update.
- Fuenzalida, D., & Mongrut, S. (2010). Estimación de tasas de descuento en Latinoamérica. Universidad del Pacífico.
- Pereiro, L. E. (2001). The valuation of closely-held companies in Latin America. *Emerging Markets Review*, 2(4), 330–370.
- Kerins, F., Smith, J. K., & Smith, R. (2004). Opportunity cost of capital for venture capital investors. *JFQA*, 39(2), 385–404.
